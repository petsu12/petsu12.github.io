About me

Description

I’m a finance student with a genuine interest in understanding how markets work, how companies make decisions, and how data can reveal the story behind the numbers. My bachelor’s studies at the University of Jyväskylä gave me a strong foundation in accounting and corporate finance, and my thesis explored cash management as a signal of external financing among European SMEs.

I’m now continuing my studies at Aalto University, where I’m focusing more deeply on asset management and corporate finance. What I enjoy most about finance is that it combines analytical thinking, business understanding, and real-world decision-making. It is not only about numbers, but also about understanding what drives companies, investors, and markets.

Alongside my studies, I’ve worked in operational and customer-facing roles that have shaped the way I work. These experiences have taught me to stay calm under pressure, pay attention to details, communicate clearly, and genuinely enjoy working with different people. I see this combination of analytical curiosity and people skills as one of my strongest assets.
